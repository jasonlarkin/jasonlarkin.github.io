Lintel Studio

This is Lintel Studio, where I teach and record music. I believe teaching and recording can be complimentary, allowing the performer to hear the details (i.e., timing, phrasing, "feel", etc.) that are hard to notice while performing, but are essential for playing a musical instrument.

My teaching style relies on developing a "good ear"; the ability to hear the essential components of music and improvise if necessary.

My recording style relies heavily on Virtual Studio Technology (VST). Lintel Studio's main tool is Apple's Logic Pro 9 Digital Audio Workstation (DAW), which contains tools such as Varispeed, Flex Time, and Flex Pitch. The VST environment allows for fast improvement of beginning and novice musical students.

VST also provides a fantastic cost-to-performance ratio. Professional quality results are possible for a wide-range of musical genres with minimal time and effort. The only "real" instruments on the sample tracks below are the guitars and most of them were produced in less than 1 days time!
